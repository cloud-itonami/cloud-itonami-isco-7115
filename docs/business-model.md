# Business Model: Independent Carpentry Practice

## Classification

- Repository: `cloud-itonami-isco-7115`
- ISCO-08: `7115`
- Occupation: Carpenters and Joiners
- Social impact: local-jobs, housing-quality, worker-safety

## Customer

- homeowners
- small contractors
- renovation clients

## Offer

- job quoting
- material planning
- cutting/assembly support
- finishing
- invoicing

## Revenue

- per-job fee
- monthly practice platform fee
- reporting package

## Trust Controls

- no robot operation near powered saws without governor gate
- working-at-height tasks require human sign-off
- job records are auditable, not editable
